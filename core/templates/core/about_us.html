Welcome to Ngawa Solutions

At Ngawa Solutions, we specialize in providing comprehensive solutions for all your technology needs. From selling high-quality laptops, desktops, printers, and accessories to offering expert repair services, we are committed to keeping your devices running smoothly.

In addition to our core services, we install advanced security fences tailored to safeguard your premises. We also take pride in our environmentally responsible approach by offering collection and disposal services for used computers and electronic equipment. Our disposal practices adhere strictly to legal standards, including the Resource Conservation & Recovery Act (RCRA) and other e-waste laws, ensuring proper recycling and minimizing environmental harm. Sensitive data is securely sanitized using techniques compliant with industry standards like the United States Department of Defense standard 5220.22-M
